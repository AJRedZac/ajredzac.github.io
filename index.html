LED ASTRAY



Edmundo Montenegro, an unlucky private investigator, receives an unexpected offer: a free stay at the luxurious Gran Hotel Villefort and a generous pay in exchange for a simple job. However, upon entering the Hotel, he realizes he has fallen into a deadly trap. Edmundo finds himself hunted by monstrous creatures and tormented by insane visions.

When all seems lost, a pair of exorcists, Abel Aburto and Nina Nava, rescue him and reveal the truth: the Villefort is infested with demons. With his sixth sense and the exorcists' help, Edmundo must confront the hotel's secrets and decipher his purpose in this sinister game before it's too late.
